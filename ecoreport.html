Waste Awareness
Learn how to segregate and manage waste responsibly
🌿

Biodegradable

Food scraps, garden waste, paper. Decomposes naturally. Compost it!

  • Vegetable peels
  • Fruit waste
  • Dry leaves
  • Tea bags
♻️

Recyclable

Plastics, glass, metals. Can be reprocessed into new products.

  • Plastic bottles
  • Glass jars
  • Newspapers
  • Cardboard
☠️

Hazardous

Dangerous to health and environment. Requires special disposal.

  • Batteries
  • Paints & solvents
  • Chemicals
  • Medical waste
💻

E-Waste

Electronic devices and components with toxic materials inside.

  • Old phones
  • Laptops & TVs
  • Wires & cables
  • Batteries

🌍 Top 10 Waste Reduction Tips

01
Carry reusable bags and avoid single-use plastics whenever possible
02
Compost kitchen waste at home using a simple composting bin
03
Segregate waste at source — don't mix categories before disposal
04
Choose products with minimal or recyclable packaging
05
Repair and reuse items instead of immediately discarding them
06
Donate or sell items you no longer need instead of throwing them away
07
Opt for digital receipts, tickets, and documents to reduce paper waste
08
Use refillable bottles and coffee cups to cut single-use plastic
09
Participate in or organize local community clean-up drives
10
Educate children and family about responsible waste disposal habits

Our Mission

EcoReport is a community-driven waste management platform built to make waste reporting easy, transparent, and rewarding. We connect citizens with waste collection teams and promote responsible disposal practices across India.

⚙️ How It Works

1
Register Sign up and create your profile
2
Report Upload waste photo and location
3
Collect Our team picks up the waste
4
Recycle Waste is sorted and processed
5
Reward Earn eco points for reporting

🛠️ Tech Stack

EcoReport is built using modern web technologies to ensure speed, reliability, and scalability across India.

React.js Node.js MongoDB Google Maps API Firebase Tailwind CSS Python AI REST API
